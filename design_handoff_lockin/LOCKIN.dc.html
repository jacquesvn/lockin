01 · DIRECTION
Instrument-panel discipline

LOCKIN reads like a pro config screen, not a gamer app. Warm-neutral ink, hairline HUD rules, monospace readouts and reticle brackets carry the tactical world — while a single hi-vis "ammo-box" yellow owns every action and win-state. Type is heavy uppercase Helvetica for scoreboard weight, system sans for body, mono for data. Quiet everywhere, loud in exactly one place. The whole product fights autopilot: the streak is a rank you defend, and ten focused minutes always beat a skipped hour.

THE RISK I TOOK
Hazard-yellow as the sole brand color. It flirts with "caution tape," but on ink it reads range-marking and ammo-spec — distinctive in a sea of neon-green/RGB gamer apps, and unmistakable at a glance in Discord.
